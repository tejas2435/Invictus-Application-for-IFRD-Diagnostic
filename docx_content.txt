THE 12 DIMENSIONS OF FUTURE READINESS™ 
The Invictus Future Readiness Diagnostic™ evaluates organisational preparedness across twelve interconnected dimensions. 
Together, these dimensions measure an organisation's ability to: 
Anticipate Change  
Interpret Complexity  
Make Better Decisions  
Adapt to Disruption  
Align Action with Intent  
These capabilities form the foundation of the Future Readiness Equation™ and the Invictus Global Future Readiness Index™ (IGFRI™).
